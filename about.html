About Me

My journey from engineering and product management to end-to-end building and visual storytelling.

The Builder Arc

I am a builder who thinks in systems. My background spans electrical engineering and product management, which taught me how to conceptualize complex applications, prioritize features, and architecture logical data flows.

Starting in 2026, I leaned heavily into the craft of AI-collaborative development. I don't outsource my thinking to AI; I treat it as a compiler for architectural plans I outline. Through this approach, I shipped multiple full-stack products solo, including a battery spoofer analyzer, a blockchain escrow application, a modular eCommerce system, and cross-platform RAG bots.

In parallel, I run Reem Studio, where I translate software architecture and product value into world-class, premium product and demo videos.
